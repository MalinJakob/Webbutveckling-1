Our Business

Freyas Photos offers a wide range of photography services, available for booking to capture all of life’s special occasions. We specialize in weddings, family portraits, maternity sessions, and children’s photography, along with freelance projects for events and personal portraits. Whether you’re looking for timeless family photos, a creative portrait session, or full wedding coverage with both photography and videography, we’re here to bring your vision to life. Our studio is dedicated to creating meaningful, high-quality images that celebrate every unique story. Book us to capture your most treasured moments, and let us help you create memories that last a lifetime.
